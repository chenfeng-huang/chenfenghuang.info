I am pleased to announce that our paper, [“Model-Agnostic Online Certificate-Driven Calibration for Time Series Forecasting Under Distribution Shift,”](https://openreview.net/forum?id=XRtUstMnAh) has been accepted as an **oral** presentation at [UAI 2026](https://www.auai.org/uai2026/) in Amsterdam (August 17-21, 2026).

Out of **1,087** submissions, **332** papers were accepted (30.5%). Of those, only **24** were selected for oral presentation, about **2.2%** of all submissions, and roughly **7.2%** of accepted papers.

I am especially grateful to my advisor, [Dr. George Michailidis](https://georgemichailidis.github.io), for his guidance and support throughout this work. I look forward to presenting in Amsterdam.
