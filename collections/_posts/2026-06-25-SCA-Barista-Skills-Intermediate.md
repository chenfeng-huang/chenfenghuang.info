Under mentor Ye Kuang’s guidance, I earned the **SCA CSP Barista Skills Intermediate** certificate. This level sits in the middle of the SCA barista track and focuses on what happens behind the bar in practice: espresso workflow and extraction control, milk texturing, latte art, drink consistency, and how station organization supports calm, repeatable service.

The course sharpened my ability to connect **technique to taste**, from dialing espresso and managing steam to pouring with intention, so the cup in front of the guest matches the standard we set for ourselves.
