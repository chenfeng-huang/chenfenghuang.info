I am glad to share that I earned the **Q Grader** certification—the industry standard for calibrated sensory evaluation and Arabica green-coffee grading.

Together with my classmates, **we were the first cohort of certified Q Graders in Chengdu**. That milestone matters for our city’s specialty coffee community: it establishes a local bench of graders who can evaluate and describe coffee against the same framework used internationally.

The Q program pushes cupping discipline, defect recognition, and scoring alignment across triangulation, roast identification, organic acids, and green grading. Finishing it was demanding and rewarding in equal measure.
