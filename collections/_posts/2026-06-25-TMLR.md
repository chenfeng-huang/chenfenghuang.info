I am pleased to announce that our paper, [“PAC-Bayesian Meta-Learning for Few-Shot Identification of Linear Dynamical Systems,”](https://openreview.net/forum?id=CiGFpSLzFv) has been accepted by [Transactions on Machine Learning Research (TMLR)](https://jmlr.org/tmlr/). The decision was **accepted as is**, with no further modifications required before the camera-ready version.

This was my **first** paper written in collaboration with my advisor, [Dr. George Michailidis](https://georgemichailidis.github.io). I really appreciate his help: his guidance shaped both the technical ideas and how I learned to develop a research project from start to finish. An extended rebuttal stretched the review timeline, so the acceptance arrived after our [second collaboration](/2026/06/02/UAI-Oral/) had already been accepted at UAI; the chronology of the news is a little inverted, but the gratitude is not.
