Under mentor Ye Kuang’s guidance, I earned the **SCA CSP Brewing-Professional** certificate. A major part of the practical work was **syphon (vacuum) brewing** on **infrared beam heaters**—managing heat stability, timing, and agitation so the upper and lower chambers behave predictably. We also worked through **pour-over drippers**, **immersion** brewers, and **batch-style** filter setups, always tying equipment choices to **grind profile, brew ratio, water quality, and extraction**. I’m looking forward to using that toolkit for more consistent recipes and clearer tasting notes in daily brewing.
